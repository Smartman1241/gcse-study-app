Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

ReviseFlow ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address through our authentication provider (Supabase).

Usage Data

We store study progress, AI usage limits, and account tier information associated with your account.

AI Questions

Questions submitted to the AI tutor are processed by OpenAI to generate responses. We do not manually review your questions unless required for security or legal reasons.

Technical Data

Our hosting provider (Vercel) may automatically collect technical information such as IP address, browser type, and device information.

Advertising

Free-tier users may see advertisements served by Google AdSense. AdSense may use cookies to provide non-personalised ads.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide account access and authentication
  • To store and sync your study data
  • To generate AI tutor responses
  • To enforce AI usage limits
  • To improve platform performance and security
  • To display advertisements for free-tier users

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party providers:

  • Supabase – authentication and database
  • OpenAI – AI response generation
  • Vercel – hosting and infrastructure
  • Google AdSense – advertising services

Each provider processes data according to their own privacy policies.

4. Cookies

We use essential cookies for login sessions. Advertising partners may use cookies to serve ads.

5. Data Retention

Your account data is stored until you delete your account. AI usage logs may be retained for service monitoring and abuse prevention.

6. Children’s Privacy

ReviseFlow is designed for students preparing for GCSE exams. If you are under 18, you should use the service with parental permission.

7. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

If you are located in the UK or EU, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request corrections
  • Request deletion of your data

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures provided by Supabase and Vercel to protect your data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
