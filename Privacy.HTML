Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

GCSE Focus ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address and encrypted authentication data through Supabase (our authentication provider).

Usage Data

We store study progress, AI usage limits, and account tier information associated with your account.

AI Input Data

Questions submitted to the AI tutor may be processed by OpenAI to generate responses. We do not manually review your questions unless required for security or legal reasons.

Technical Data

We may collect technical information such as IP address, browser type, and device information through our hosting provider (Vercel).

Advertising Data

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements to free-tier users. AdSense may use cookies and similar technologies to provide non-personalised ads.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide account access and authentication
  • To store study data and AI usage information
  • To generate AI tutor responses
  • To improve service performance and security
  • To display advertisements for free-tier users

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to operate GCSE Focus:

  • Supabase – authentication and database hosting
  • OpenAI – AI tutor response generation
  • Vercel – website hosting
  • Google AdSense – advertising services

Each provider may process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

4. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. Advertising partners such as Google may use cookies to serve ads.

5. Data Retention

Your account data is stored until you delete your account. AI usage logs are stored for service operation and abuse prevention.

6. Children's Privacy

GCSE Focus is designed for students preparing for GCSE examinations. We do not knowingly collect unnecessary personal data from users under 18. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided inappropriate personal information, please contact us.

7. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

If you are located in the United Kingdom or EU, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent where applicable

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures provided by Supabase and Vercel to protect your data.
